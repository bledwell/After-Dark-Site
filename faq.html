Frequently Asked Questions

General

Who is After Dark for?

After Dark is designed for Canvas LMS administrators — people who need to search, audit, and compare courses and users across an entire institution. It is not intended for faculty or students.

Does After Dark store my Canvas data?

No. After Dark routes all requests through your existing Canvas browser session. Data is fetched live from Canvas and displayed in the extension — nothing is sent to external servers or stored beyond your local browser session. See the Privacy & Security page for full details.

Does After Dark work outside the UW System?

Yes. After Dark works with any Canvas LMS instance. The UW System themes are cosmetic — the tool itself connects to whatever Canvas URL you configure in the setup wizard.

Which browsers are supported?

After Dark works in Chrome and any Chromium-based browser including Microsoft Edge, Brave, and Arc. It does not work in Firefox or Safari.

Technical

After Dark can't connect to Canvas. What do I do?

Make sure you have a Canvas tab open and are logged in before opening After Dark. The extension routes requests through your active Canvas session — if no Canvas tab is open, it has nothing to connect through. Reload the Canvas tab and try again.

Search results aren't showing up.

Try reloading your Canvas tab. After Dark injects a small content script into Canvas pages to handle authentication — if the tab was open before After Dark was installed, it may need a reload to pick up the script.

Can I use After Dark with a vanity URL?

Yes. During setup (or in Settings afterwards) you can enter both a primary Canvas URL and a vanity URL. After Dark will recognize Canvas tabs on either URL.
