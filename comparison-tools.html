Comparison Tools

Compare courses, environments, blueprint families, and people side by side. See exactly what differs and where.

Course Comparison

Select any two courses and compare their content side by side. Every module, assignment, quiz, discussion, page, and file is listed with a diff badge showing whether it's the same, different, or missing from one side. Works within a single environment or across Live, Test, and Beta.

Course Comparison — side-by-side content diff including cross-environment comparison.

Running a comparison

  1. Search for a course and click Compare on its card, or use the Compare tab directly.
  2. Select a second course from the search results.
  3. The comparison view loads both courses with their full content tree.
  4. Diff badges appear on every item — Same, Different, or Not in destination.
  5. Use the section filters to focus on specific content types.

Cross-environment comparison

To compare the same course across Live and Test (or Beta), make sure you have both Canvas environments open in separate browser tabs. After Dark will detect both environments and let you select which one to pull each course from.

Tip: Cross-environment comparison is useful before a major course redesign — compare your Test version against Live to confirm what changed before pushing to students.

Blueprint Family View

Browse your Blueprint master courses and all their associated child courses. See sync status, last sync date, and compare any child against the master or against each other.

Blueprint Family View — master courses, children, and sync status.
  1. Search for a Blueprint master course and click its card.
  2. The Family View shows all associated child courses with their sync status.
  3. Click any child course to open it in the audit view.
  4. Use Compare to diff a child against the master, or two children against each other.
Note: Blueprint Family View is only available for courses flagged as Blueprint masters in Canvas.

People Compare & Similar Activity

Compare two Canvas users side by side — their enrollments, page view history, and activity patterns. The Similar Activity feature highlights matching page views within a configurable time window, making it straightforward to identify students working in lockstep.

People Compare and Similar Activity — comparing two users' Canvas activity.

Running a people comparison

  1. Search for the first person and click Add to Compare.
  2. Search for the second person and add them.
  3. Click Compare to open the side-by-side view.
  4. Switch between the Enrollments and Page Views tabs — both columns switch simultaneously.

Using Similar Activity

  1. Switch both columns to the Page Views tab.
  2. Click the 🔗 Similar Activity button in the header.
  3. Matching rows are highlighted amber. Non-matching rows are dimmed.
  4. Use the ▾ arrow to change the time window (1, 5, 10, 15, 30, or 60 minutes).
  5. The activity timeline below both columns shows the pattern visually — dots represent page views, amber vertical lines mark matches.
  6. Click any amber dot in the timeline to jump directly to that row and highlight it.
Academic integrity: Similar Activity is not evidence on its own — it's a starting point for investigation. Use it alongside submission timestamps and grading data for a fuller picture.
