Search Tools

Find any course or person in Canvas in seconds. Filter, browse, and drill into details without leaving After Dark.

Course Search People Search & Compare User Search

Course Search

Course Search is the heart of After Dark. Search across your entire Canvas instance by course name, instructor name, or section number. Filter by term, sub-account, publish status, and enrollment count.

Course Search — finding courses by name, instructor, and section number.

How to search

  1. Click the Courses tab at the top of After Dark.
  2. Select a term from the term dropdown, or leave it on “All Terms” to search everything.
  3. Type a course name, instructor name, or section number in the search box.
  4. Results appear as cards showing the course name, term, sub-account, instructor, enrollment count, and publish status.
  5. Click any card to open the Course Audit view for that course.

Filters

Use the filter bar to narrow results by:

  • Term — limit to a specific term or academic year
  • Sub-account — filter by department or college
  • Publish status — Published, Unpublished, or All
  • Enrollment — filter by student count range
Tip: Searching by section number is especially useful for quickly finding which course a student is registered in when all you have is a section code from the registrar.

People Search & Compare

Find any Canvas user by name or login ID. View their full enrollment history grouped by term, page view activity, last login, and profile details. Compare two users side by side to spot patterns.

People Search & Compare — finding users and comparing their activity.

Finding a person

  1. Click the People tab at the top of After Dark.
  2. Type a name, email, or login ID in the search box.
  3. Select the role filter (All, Teacher, Student) if needed.
  4. Click a result card to open the person's detail view.

Person detail view

The detail view shows the person's profile information, all enrollments grouped by term, and their page view history for the last 30 days. Page views are grouped by course, showing which objects (quizzes, assignments, discussions) they accessed and when.

Comparing two people

  1. Search for the first person and click Add to the comparison tray.
  2. Search for the second person and add them too.
  3. Click Compare to open the side-by-side view.
  4. Both columns show enrollments and page views simultaneously.
  5. Use the Similar Activity button to highlight matching page views within a configurable time window.
Academic integrity: The Similar Activity feature highlights rows where both users accessed the same Canvas URL on the same day within your chosen time window (1–60 minutes). The activity timeline below the columns shows the pattern visually — click any dot to jump to that row.

User Search

A focused search for finding Canvas users across all roles. Useful for quickly looking up a specific person when you know their name or login but don't need the full comparison workflow.

User Search — finding and reviewing individual Canvas users.
  1. Click the People tab and search by name or login ID.
  2. Use the role filter to narrow to Teachers, Students, or All.
  3. Click a result to view the person's full profile, enrollments, and page views.
  4. From the profile view, click any course name to open that course in the After Dark audit view.
